During

Don't panic. That is the number 1 cause of injuries during an earthquake.

Drop on knees and hands to avoid falling.

Cover. Find your safe spot and crawl to it. Cover your head and neck.

Hold on.

Don't stand in a doorway, run outside, or "triangle of life". These are considered dangerous and are not recommended.
